Now

This page details what I am currently doing.

I am studying Computer Science as part of a Masters Conversion program in University College Dublin, due to graduate in December 2023. I am an active member in the UCD Mountaineering Club having been the Health and Safety Officer in 21/22. This means I spend 2-3 evenings a week climbing at the UCD Wall. Whilst on the weekends I can be found hiking in Wicklow or on a club trip to other mountain regions in Ireland.

In the mornings I usually do yoga with the Down Dog app. It is free for students to use. Afterwards I use Sam Harris' meditation app Waking Up. I have found Waking Up to be a very valuable resource and have written more about it here.
